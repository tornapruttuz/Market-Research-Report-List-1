<p><ul><li>Tubular Retractors</li><li>Blade Retractor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal retractor market comprises two main types: tubular retractors and blade retractors. Tubular retractors are designed for minimally invasive surgeries, allowing precise access to the surgical site while minimizing tissue disruption. They are favored for their ability to improve patient recovery times. In contrast, blade retractors are traditional devices used in more invasive procedures, providing wider exposure and stability during surgery but typically requiring larger incisions. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing surgical outcomes in spinal procedures.</p></p>
