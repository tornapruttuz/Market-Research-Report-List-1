<p><p>The overall flare system market comprises three primary types: air-assisted, steam-assisted, and pressure-assisted flares. Air-assisted flares utilize a combination of air and fuel for combustion, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions. Steam-assisted flares use steam to promote combustion and minimize smoke production, making them environmentally friendly. Pressure-assisted flares rely on high-pressure gas to enhance the combustion process, offering high efficiency and reliability. These systems are essential in managing emissions from industrial operations, ensuring safety and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
