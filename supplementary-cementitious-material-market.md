<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Supplementary Cementitious Material (SCM) market primarily consists of two types: solid and powder forms. Solid SCMs, such as fly ash and slag, are often used in bulk applications, providing improved durability and strength to concrete. In contrast, powder SCMs, like silica fume and metakaolin, are finely ground materials, enhancing the performance of concrete in terms of workability and strength at lower dosages. Both types contribute to sustainable construction practices by reducing cement consumption and minimizing carbon footprints.</p></p>
