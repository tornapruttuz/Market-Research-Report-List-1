<p><p>Flat roof waterproofing systems consist of various types designed to prevent water ingress and extend roof lifespan. Waterproof membranes, often made from synthetic materials, provide a durable barrier. Reinforced asphalt films combine asphalt with reinforcing elements for added strength. Monolayer films offer a single-layer application for simplicity and efficiency. Liquid roofs involve applying liquid coatings that cure to form a seamless membrane, while Frankincense asphalt systems utilize specially formulated asphalt for enhanced waterproofing and flexibility, catering to diverse roofing requirements.</p></p>
