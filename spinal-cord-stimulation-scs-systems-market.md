<p><p>Spinal Cord Stimulation (SCS) Systems are medical devices designed to alleviate chronic pain by delivering electrical impulses to the spinal cord. Radiofrequency stimulation uses thermal energy to modulate pain signals without implanting electrodes. Other types may include newer technologies like closed-loop systems, which adapt stimulation based on real-time feedback. Each type targets chronic pain management, offering varying approaches based on patient needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
