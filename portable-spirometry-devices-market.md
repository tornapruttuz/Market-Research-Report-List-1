<p><p>The portable spirometry devices market includes three main types: flow-sensing spirometry devices that measure the rate of airflow during inhalation and exhalation, providing critical information for diagnosing respiratory conditions. Peak flow spirometry devices specifically assess the peak expiratory flow rate, aiding in asthma management. Volume measurement spirometry devices determine the total volume of air inhaled and exhaled, offering insights into lung function. Together, these devices enhance respiratory monitoring and facilitate timely interventions for patients with chronic respiratory issues.</p></p>
