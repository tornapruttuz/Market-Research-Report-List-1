<p><p>The Ultraviolet Band Fiber Optical Spectrometer market is primarily divided into two types: Single Mode Fiber Spectrometers and Multimode Fiber Spectrometers. Single Mode Fiber Spectrometers are designed for precise measurements and high sensitivity, typically used in applications requiring long-range transmission and minimal signal loss. Both types serve unique needs in spectral analysis.</p></p>
