<p><p>The FTIR gas analyzer market is segmented into high concentration range and low concentration range analyzers. High concentration range FTIR analyzers operate effectively at parts per million (ppm) levels, making them ideal for applications with significant gas emissions, such as industrial processes. In contrast, low concentration range FTIR analyzers detect gases at parts per billion (ppb) and ppm levels, suitable for monitoring trace gases in environmental studies and regulatory compliance. Each type serves specific analytical requirements across various industries.</p></p>
