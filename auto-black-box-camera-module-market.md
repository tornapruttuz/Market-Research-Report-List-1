<p><p>The Auto Black Box Camera Module market primarily consists of two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras are known for their high image quality and sensitivity, making them effective in low-light conditions. In contrast, CMOS technology offers lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integrated functionalities, leading to cost-effective solutions. Both types are widely used in automotive applications for surveillance, accident recording, and driver assistance systems, enhancing safety and security on the road.</p></p>
