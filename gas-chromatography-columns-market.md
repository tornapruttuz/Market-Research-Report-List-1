<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Gas Chromatography Columns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wall Coated Open Tubular (WCOT)</li><li>Surface Coated Open Tubular (SCOT)</li><li>Fused Silica Open Tubular (FSOT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas chromatography columns market can be categorized into three main types: Wall Coated Open Tubular (WCOT) columns, which feature a stationary phase coated on the inner wall of the tube for efficient separation; Surface Coated Open Tubular (SCOT) columns that utilize a stationary phase adhered to the tube's surface, enhancing sensitivity; and Fused Silica Open Tubular (FSOT) columns made from high-purity fused silica, providing durability and flexibility, ideal for a variety of analytical applications.</p></p>
