<p><p>The Smart Digital Stethoscope market is categorized into two main types: wireless stethoscopes and wired stethoscopes. Wireless stethoscopes utilize Bluetooth or Wi-Fi connectivity to transmit sounds directly to smartphones or tablets, allowing for enhanced portability and recording capabilities. In contrast, wired stethoscopes rely on traditional cables to connect to recording devices, offering a reliable and straightforward option for healthcare professionals. Both types serve the essential function of auscultation but cater to different preferences in terms of convenience and technology integration.</p></p>
