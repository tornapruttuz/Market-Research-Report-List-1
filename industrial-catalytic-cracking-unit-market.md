<p><p>The Industrial Catalytic Cracking Unit market consists of two primary types: high parallel and low parallel, as well as coaxial configurations. High parallel units emphasize efficiency and throughput, enabling the processing of higher volumes of crude oil into valuable products like gasoline and diesel. Low parallel units, in contrast, focus on lower feed rates while maintaining effectiveness in refining. Coaxial configurations integrate both high and low pressures for optimized performance, enhancing flexibility and operational efficiency within the catalytic cracking process.</p></p>
