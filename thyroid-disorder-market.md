<p><ul><li>Hypothyroidism</li><li>Hyperthyroidism</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thyroid disorder market is primarily categorized into two main types: hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism. Hypothyroidism involves insufficient thyroid hormone production, leading to symptoms like fatigue, weight gain, and depression, and requires lifelong management often with hormone replacement therapy. In contrast, hyperthyroidism is characterized by excessive hormone production, causing symptoms such as weight loss, anxiety, and increased heart rate, which may be treated through medications, radioactive iodine, or surgery. Both markets are growing due to rising awareness and improved diagnostic methods.</p></p>
