<p><p>The Near Infrared (NIR) spectrometer market includes several types, each suited for specific applications. FT-NIR (Fourier Transform NIR) is favored for its speed and high resolution, ideal for complex sample analysis. UV-Vis-NIR combines ultraviolet and visible light detection for a broader spectral range, useful in chemical and material characterization. Raman spectrometers employ inelastic scattering for detailed molecular information, often utilized in pharmaceuticals and materials science. Other variants exist, catering to niche applications and technologies in various industries.</p></p>
