<p><p>Electrophoresis technology is a widely used technique for the separation and analysis of macromolecules such as proteins, nucleic acids, and polysaccharides based on their size and charge. This method utilizes an electric field to drive charged particles through a gel or solution, enabling researchers to study molecular structures, functions, and interactions. The technology is extensively utilized in various applications, including genomics, proteomics, and diagnostics, making it a crucial component of modern biochemistry and molecular biology.</p><p>The Electrophoresis Technology Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period. Key trends include the development of new and innovative electrophoresis devices, integration with computer software for advanced analysis, and growing applications in the food and environmental sectors to ensure quality control and safety.
