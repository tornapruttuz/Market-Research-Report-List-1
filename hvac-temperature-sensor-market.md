<p><ul><li>NTC Sensor</li><li>PTC Sensor</li><li>RTDSensor</li><li>Thermocouples (TCs) Sensor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVAC temperature sensor market includes various types suited for different applications. NTC sensors, with negative temperature coefficient characteristics, are widely used for precise temperature measurement. PTC sensors, featuring positive temperature coefficients, serve as self-regulating heaters. RTD sensors offer high accuracy and stability, typically using platinum elements. Thermocouples (TCs) provide a robust solution for extreme temperatures, often in industrial settings. Other sensors may include infrared and semiconductor-based options, catering to specialized needs across HVAC systems for efficient climate control.</p></p>
