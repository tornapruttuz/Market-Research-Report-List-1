<p><p>Night guards for teeth grinding, or bruxism, come in three main types: soft mouth guards, hard mouth guards, and double laminate mouth guards. Soft mouth guards are flexible and provide comfort while absorbing mild grinding. Hard mouth guards, made from rigid materials, offer greater durability and are suitable for severe grinding. Double laminate mouth guards combine both soft and hard materials, providing a balance of comfort and protection. Each type caters to different severity levels and individual preferences, enhancing oral health during sleep.</p></p>
