<p><p>Embolectomy catheters are specialized medical devices used to remove emboli, or blood clots, from the vascular system. The market for these catheters is categorized primarily into two types: single lumen and double lumen. Single lumen catheters have a single channel, allowing for simple suction of clots, while double lumen catheters feature two channels, enabling simultaneous suction and infusion, which can enhance the efficiency of clot removal. Each type serves specific clinical needs, influencing their application in interventional procedures.</p></p>
