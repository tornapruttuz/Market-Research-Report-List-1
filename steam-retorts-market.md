<p><strong>The Steam Retorts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Retorts</li><li>Rotary Retorts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam retorts market is primarily segmented into static and rotary retorts. Static retorts are ideal for processing small batches, as they maintain a consistent temperature and pressure, ensuring even heat distribution for easily stackable containers. Rotary retorts, on the other hand, feature a cylindrical design that rotates during processing, promoting uniform heat exposure and reducing cook times. Both types are vital in food and pharmaceutical industries for sterilization and preservation, catering to varying production needs and efficiencies.</p></p>
