<p><p>Oil and gas separation systems are crucial for efficiently separating hydrocarbon mixtures into oil, gas, and water phases. Horizontal separators utilize gravity to separate these phases in a long tank, ideal for handling large volumes. Vertical separators are cylindrical and excel in areas with limited space, offering effective separation through gravity and centrifugal forces. Spherical separators, with their compact design, enhance efficiency by minimizing pressure drop and maximizing surface area, making them effective for offshore applications and tight installations.</p></p>
