<p><p>The Hipot Tester market is categorized into AC Hipot Testers and DC Hipot Testers, each serving distinct applications in electrical testing. AC Hipot Testers apply alternating current to evaluate insulation integrity and dielectric breakdown, making them essential for testing devices like transformers and cables. Conversely, DC Hipot Testers utilize direct current for testing, providing precise insulation assessment for electronic components and circuits. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety and compliance in electrical equipment across various industries.</p></p>
