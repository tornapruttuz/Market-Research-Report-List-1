<p><p>The Automated Medication Dispensing Cabinet market is categorized into centralized and decentralized dispensing systems. Centralized dispensing cabinets are typically located in pharmacy areas, allowing for bulk storage and controlled access to medications, enhancing inventory management and security. In contrast, decentralized dispensing cabinets are situated closer to the point of care, enabling nurses and healthcare providers to access medications quickly and conveniently, thereby improving patient care efficiency and reducing wait times. Both systems ultimately aim to streamline medication management in healthcare settings.</p></p>
