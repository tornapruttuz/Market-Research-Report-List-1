<p><p>The HiFi headphone market encompasses three main types: in-ear, on-ear, and over-ear headphones. On-ear headphones rest on the ears, providing a balance of comfort and sound quality without complete isolation. Over-ear headphones envelop the ears entirely, delivering superior sound quality and comfort, often favored for home listening.
