<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Photolithography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV (i-line)</li><li>DUV (KrF, ArF Dry, ArFi)</li><li>EUV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photolithography equipment encompasses various technologies used in semiconductor manufacturing. UV lithography, particularly i-line, utilizes ultraviolet light for patterning, suitable for less complex circuits. DUV lithography, which includes KrF and ArF technologies, employs shorter wavelengths to achieve finer resolutions, with ArFi offering enhanced imaging for advanced nodes. EUV lithography represents the cutting edge, using extreme ultraviolet light to enable the production of smaller features at advanced technology nodes, thus enabling the fabrication of next-generation semiconductors.</p></p>
