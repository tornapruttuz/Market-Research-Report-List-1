<p><p>The medical gauze and tapes market comprises various types used for wound care and dressing. Woven gauze, made from interlaced fibers, offers breathability and absorbency, ideal for covering larger wounds. Non-woven gauze is crafted from synthetic fibers, providing a softer touch and reduced linting, suitable for delicate skin. Fabric tapes offer flexibility and strength for securing dressings, while paper tapes are hypoallergenic and gentle, ideal for sensitive skin. Plastic tapes are water-resistant and durable, making them suitable for various medical applications.</p></p>
