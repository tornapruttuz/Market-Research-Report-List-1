<p><strong>The Quench Cooler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Phase Quenching</li><li>Air Quenching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quench Cooler market primarily comprises two types: Liquid Phase Quenching and Air Quenching. Liquid Phase Quenching involves immersing materials in a quenching liquid, such as water or oil, to rapidly cool them, leading to improved hardness and strength. In contrast, Air Quenching utilizes ambient air to cool materials, offering a gentler process that minimizes warping and cracking. Both methods are essential in heat treatment applications, catering to various industrial requirements based on material properties and desired outcomes.</p></p>
