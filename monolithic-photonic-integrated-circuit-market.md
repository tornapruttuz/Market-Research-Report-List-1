<p><strong>The Monolithic Photonic Integrated Circuit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit</li><li>Active Photonic Integrated Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The monolithic photonic integrated circuit market is segmented into two main types: passive and active photonic integrated circuits. Passive photonic integrated circuits comprise components like waveguides and optical filters that require no external energy source, functioning purely through the manipulation of light. In contrast, active photonic integrated circuits incorporate elements such as lasers and modulators, which rely on electrical energy to control light emissions and signal processing. Both types play crucial roles in advancing telecommunications, sensing, and data processing technologies.</p></p>
