<p><p>The marine wiper system market features two primary types: single pendulum systems and dual pendulum systems. Single pendulum systems utilize one arm that sweeps in a semicircular motion, providing efficient visibility on smaller vessels. In contrast, dual pendulum systems incorporate two arms to enhance coverage and adaptability on larger ships, ensuring a wider wiping area. Both systems are designed for reliability and performance, addressing the unique needs of marine environments while improving safety and navigation visibility under adverse weather conditions.</p></p>
