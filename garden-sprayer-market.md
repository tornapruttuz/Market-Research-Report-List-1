<p><p>The garden sprayer market is divided into two main types: manual control and electric control. In contrast, electric control sprayers use a motor to generate pressure, offering ease of use and efficiency for larger areas. They often feature adjustable nozzles and longer operating times, catering to diverse gardening needs and user preferences in spraying tasks.</p></p>
