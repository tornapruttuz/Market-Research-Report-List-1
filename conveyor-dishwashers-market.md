<p><p>The conveyor dishwashers market includes two primary types: rack-type and conveyor-type dishwashers. Conveyor-type dishwashers, on the other hand, feature a continuous belt system that allows for high-capacity washing, suitable for large-scale operations like restaurants and catering services.
